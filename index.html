ROOSY SHOP

We are a family run business who take pride in fulfilling our customers floral requirements to the highest possible standard. With over 30 years experience, our friendly trained staff are always available on the telephone and face to face, to help and advise and we always take personal pride in every order. We know that choosing the right flowers for the right occasion can sometimes be rather daunting, so let us make your life easier. We have developed this website to make ordering flowers on line an easy and secure experience; all you need is to browse through the categories and order. If you would like help ordering please feel free to call. Our services expand across all aspects of the floral industry. Specialising in weddings, funeral tributes, corporate supply or just simply a bouquet to a loved one. All valentine deliveries will be delivered in working hours, if you special delivery instructions there is a box at the end on the order to detail these.
